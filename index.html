Welcome to Out of Touch!

Out of Touch is a company that provides space exploration simulations. You can go to Mars, Jupiter, and Neptune. Once you select your simulation of choice, you will then have to prepare for your simulation by selecting supplies such as sleeping, clothing and hygiene necessities, transportation method, and a few optional equipments you may find necessary for survival. Once you've finished prep, you will then be transported to you planet of choice via simulation. The simulation will last anywhere from 2-3 months depending on which planet you choose. Each planet has it's share of difficulties, including extraterrestrial entities. Much about life on other planets is unknown, and it's up to you to do the unimaginable; things most people would consider out of touch.

For those who are successful, they are more than deserving of an reward. The purpose of the simulations are not only for fun, but to give the most clever, risky, and willing to pay the price, the chance to explore these plants in real life. If you are able to survive the simulation, you will then advance to the in person consultation with one of our consultants who will take you through the legalities, and financial requirements to go to your planet. Before going, you must complete 6 months od training to ensure you are properly prepared for your journey. Once you have completed trainng and setteled the financial and legal measures, you are then ready for take off. Your journey will last 1-2 months and don't worry, you won't be alone. We have trained professionals who will accompnay you and assist you as you indulge in your reward!
